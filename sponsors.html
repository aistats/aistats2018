Call for Sponsors

All sponsorship queries must be directed to Simon Lacoste-Julien,[aistats2018.sponsor@gmail.com].

The speakers and participants at AISTATS traditionally number some of the most prestigious researchers in the above fields, together with their students and the most promising young talent. We invite contributions from your organization to sponsor AISTATS 2018. Sponsorship brings with it the following key benefits:
  • Direct outreach to experts from industry and academia
  • Disbursal of merchandise, information brochures, souvenirs
  • Special recognition within the community
  • Direct outreach to top quality students and young researchers
To help determine the level of contribution, we welcome sponsorship at 4 different levels. Sponsorship provides funds that help ensure the success of the conference. It is also an effective means of advertisement throughout the international machine learning community. The majority of contributions support registration and travel costs for students and young researchers.

All sponsorship levels include recognition on the conference website, including a logo and link of your choice, company logo on the conference program, 1 full conference registration (including the conference banquet), and poster board space during the evening poster sessions (2- 3 evenings).

  • Posterboard space during evening poster sessions (2-3 evenings)

  • Sponsorship of coffee breaks (includes listing in program, registration, plus posterboard space during breaks)
